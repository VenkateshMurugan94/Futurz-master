The popularity of the portal is evident from the fact that it has crossed the 2.5 crore candidate landmark and has more than 3 lakh latest job vacancies from leading companies on the site.

FUTURZ.com connects jobseekers and recruiters by accurately matching candidate profiles to the relevant job openings through an advanced 2-way matching technology. While most job portals only focus on getting candidates the next job, FUTURZ focuses on the entire career growth of candidates. To this end, FUTURZ has launched FUTURZ Learning- Indias largest career skills site for working professionals with over 500+ courses & certifications.

As the industry shifts towards mobile, FUTURZ.com is leading the transition and is the fastest growing job portal on mobile devices, witnessing a 100% YOY growth in mobile traffic and also offers on-the-go jobs through the FUTURZ Job Search App.

While FUTURZ HR Conclave brings top HR leaders to share insights on latest trends, innovations & best practices in the HR industry.
